Chana Masala

Description

Chana masala is a popular Indian curry made with kidney beans, tomatoes, onions, and spices. It is a flavorful and aromatic dish that is often served with rice or naan.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup chopped green chillies
  • 1/2 cup chopped garlic
  • 1/2 cup chopped ginger
  • 1/2 cup chopped coriander
  • 1/2 cup chopped chilli powder
  • 1/2 cup chopped turmeric
  • 1/2 cup chopped cumin
  • 1/2 cup chopped paprika

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions, tomatoes, and garlic.
  2. Add spices and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add kidney beans and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Serve with rice or naan.
