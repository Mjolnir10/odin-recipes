Carrot Halwa

Description

Served warm, gajar ka halwa (carrot halwa) is a sweet pudding of simmered carrots, milk, sugar, and cardamom. There are several types of halwa in South Asia and this carrot halwa is especially popular during both Diwali and Eid.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup grated carrots
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 cup cardamom powder

Steps

  1. Heat 1/2 cup water in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Add 1/2 cup milk and 1/2 cup sugar.
  3. Stir until sugar dissolves.
  4. Add 1/2 cup grated carrots and stir.
  5. Simmer over medium heat until carrots are tender, about 15 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat.
  7. Stir in 1/4 cup cardamom powder.
  8. Stir in 1/2 cup water.
